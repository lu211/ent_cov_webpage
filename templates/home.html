Analysing Entities in News Summarization

This website was built to visualize the entity coverage of news article in comparison with their summaries.

The data used here is retrieved from the corpora of CNN/Dailymail and XSum (BBC) which contains many articles and their reference summaries, mined from the named newspaper. An entity analysis was processed over this data, as well as automatic summarization of the articles was created, by using up-to-date models for abstractive summarization. The models used here are T5, Pegasus and BART.

Have fun exploring!
